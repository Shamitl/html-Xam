Gran Turismo

Yes, I know, How can you make a movie from a racing game with no story?
Well how about telling a real life story of a gamer boy who's dream is to become a profesional racer. This movie tells the story of a boy who is good at Gran Turismo and gets peeked to earn the chance to race for Nissan as a professional racer.

The story is really well told. And the script is interesting but what makes the movie really entertaining are the actors, Orlando Bloom and the guy from Stranger Things. They make you feel what they are feelling across the screen and not a lot of actors can do that, you can feel the emotion, the exaitment and the fear that a car race can give you.
